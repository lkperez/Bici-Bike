About Us

Bicibike is a student project made by Jillian Lehosky, Laisha Koon-Perez, Kika Kovaleski, and Rachel Tolentino. Embrace the environment, use bici because you're busy! Personal autmobiles are expensive, bad for the environment, and inconvenient for the 35,000 students who call Nipmuc Notch home for 9 months of the year. With Bicibike, you gain instant access to the world arounf you, all at an affordable price
